TapKrup Icons Folder

Save your icons and favicons here:

Required files:
- favicon.ico (16x16, 32x32, 48x48)
- icon.png (512x512)
- apple-touch-icon.png (180x180)

Optional:
- icon-192.png (192x192 for PWA)
- icon-512.png (512x512 for PWA)

After adding icons, update src/app/layout.tsx to reference them.
